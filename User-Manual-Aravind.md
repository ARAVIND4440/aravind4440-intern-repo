## How I Work Best

I do my best work when I have clear instructions and a structured approach to tasks. Breaking work into smaller steps helps me stay focused and productive.

I struggle when tasks are unclear or when there are too many things happening at once without proper direction.

## Communication Style

I prefer messages to be clear and direct, with enough detail so I can understand what is expected.

The best way to reach me is through messages (Slack/Discord/email), as I can respond quickly and keep track of conversations.

If something is urgent, please send a direct message so I can prioritise it immediately.

I also prefer an Quick call for an serious escalation

## Collaboration & Teamwork

I work best in a team when communication is clear and everyone is aligned on goals.

My biggest pet peeve in teamwork is unclear instructions or last-minute changes without proper context.

If I seem quiet in a meeting, it usually means I am listening carefully and processing the discussion before contributing.

## Focus & Productivity

I stay focused by working on one task at a time and following a structured plan.

Things that distract me include constant interruptions or switching between too many tasks.

The best way to support my productivity is to provide clear priorities and allow uninterrupted time for deep work.

## Feedback & Growth

I like feedback to be clear, direct, and constructive, with examples so I can improve.

When I make a mistake, the best way to let me know is by explaining what went wrong and how I can fix it.

I appreciate it when teammates give honest feedback and guidance to help me grow.

## Fun Facts & Quirks
