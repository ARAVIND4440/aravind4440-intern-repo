## Research & Learn

## What AI tools are typically used for your role?

As a data science student and intern, I use AI tools like ChatGPT mainly to support my learning and productivity. For example, I use it to understand difficult concepts, debug code, and get a starting point for writing documentation or reports. It also helps me explore ideas during data analysis.

## What are the benefits and risks of using AI in a professional setting?

### Benefits:
- Saves time when working on tasks or learning new topics  
- Helps me understand concepts more quickly  
- Useful for brainstorming and problem-solving  

### Risks:
- Sometimes gives incorrect or incomplete information  
- Risk of accidentally sharing sensitive data  
- Can lead to over-reliance if used without thinking critically  

## What types of information should never be entered into AI tools?

- Company or project confidential data  
- Personal or user information  
- Passwords, API keys, or credentials  
- Any internal details that are not meant to be shared publicly  

## How can you fact-check and validate AI-generated content to ensure accuracy?

I understand that AI is not always accurate, so I make sure to:
- Double-check information using trusted sources  
- Review and edit the output before using it  
- Test any code provided  
- Use my own understanding to confirm the results 

## Reflection

## When should you use AI for assistance, and when should you rely on your own skills?

I use AI as a support tool when I need guidance or want to improve efficiency. However, I rely on my own skills when making final decisions, solving problems, and validating results. This helps me actually learn instead of just depending on AI.

## How can you avoid over-reliance on AI while still benefiting from it?

To avoid becoming too dependent on AI, I first try to solve problems on my own. I only use AI when I need help or a different perspective. This approach helps me build my confidence and improve my skills over time.

## What steps will you take to ensure data privacy when using AI tools?

I make sure not to share any sensitive or confidential data while using AI tools. I also follow company guidelines and only use AI for safe and appropriate tasks.
